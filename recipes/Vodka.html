Vodka Lemonade with Mint

This is a cool and refreshing adult vodka lemonade drink for summertime, or any time! Garnish with mint sprigs.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • ⅔ cup white sugar
  • 12 fluid ounces vodka
  • ½ fluid ounce lime juice
  • 1 cup fresh lemon juice
  • crushed ice

Directions

  1. Combine chopped mint and sugar in a large bowl. Stir in vodka and lemon juice. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours.
  2. Strain lemonade into a pitcher. Fill six 6- to 8-ounce glasses with crushed ice; pour lemonade over.
